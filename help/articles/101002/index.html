Requesting Account Data

GDPR Compliance & Personal Privacy Checkup Request

Important!

Please note that the data in your file will not be encrypted. This means that if you send your file to anyone or you leak it to the public, people may be able to gain illicit access to your account!


How to Request Your Data

Firstly, make sure you are logged in on the official Hydraulisc App/Site! This guide covers the official instance only!

  1. Once logged in, navigate to your profile.
  2. From your profile, navigate to your settings.
  3. In the main list of settings, click on the "Privacy" option.
  4. Scroll down until you see the "Personal Pricacy Checkup Request", located near the bottom, and click it.
  5. Follow along with any additional prompts to request your data.

Please note, these instructions work on all Official Hydraulisc Instances, ranging from the Desktop Site & PWA to the (invite-only) HMK variation, and everything in-between.
